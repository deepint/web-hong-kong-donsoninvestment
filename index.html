Hong Kong Donson Investment Limited

Hong Kong Donson Investment Limited is a company registered in Hong Kong on December 5, 2023. Its primary business is investment, focusing on unregulated financial services and investment activities.

Private Equity Investment (Unregulated Portion)

Engaging in equity investments in unlisted companies, without involving public fundraising or securities trading.

Participating in projects through direct investments or establishing Limited Partnership (LP) funds.

Cross-border Investment and Asset Management

Assisting clients with overseas asset allocation, such as investments in real estate, commodities, art, and other non-financial assets.

Business Consulting and Strategic Planning

Providing consulting services for market analysis, business model design, and strategic planning.

Assisting clients in expanding international markets or optimizing operational efficiency.

Cross-border Investment Platform

Leveraging Hong Kong's international status to establish a cross-border investment platform, helping mainland clients invest in overseas assets.

Corporate Incubation and Investment

Investing in startups, providing funding and strategic support to help them grow and achieve an exit.

Real Estate Investment and Management

Engaging in real estate investments in Hong Kong and abroad, offering asset management and leasing services.

Green Finance and Sustainable Development

Participating in emerging fields such as green bonds, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investments, etc.

Cultural and Technology Investment

Investing in the cultural industry and technological innovation projects to promote the development of high-potential sectors.
